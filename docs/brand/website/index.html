About BeckCloud

A homelab with opinions

BeckCloud is a personal homelab built around K3s running on OpenNebula. It's not a startup, not a product, and definitely not a SaaS. It's one person's attempt to make the kind of infrastructure they'd want to use — because the cloud providers' terms of service always come first.

The stack runs roughly twenty services across a small Kubernetes cluster, orchestrated with Flux for GitOps. Everything from identity management to monitoring, from backups to development tools. together with Cilium for networking, Traefik for routing, and Grafana for making sure nothing's on fire.

It's complicated. It shouldn't be this complicated. But it works, and understanding exactly how it works — and fixing it when it doesn't — is the point.
